Stickers

Free stickers are available for all users to download but there are also paid stickers for any Line fans who can't get enough of these expressive stickers. Some are still-photos while others are animated and are available with sound effects.

Games

Line also develops game application that are free to download. You can play games with friends and share achievements on your Line timeline. Ocassionally, game downloads include free stickers or free coins.

Utility

Aside from games, Line also provides utility application such as Aillis (formerly Line Camera), a photo enchancement app and Line Shop, an online market for users to sell their goods etc.

Themes

If you are not happy with the way Line Interface looks, you can choose to buy from a wide selection of Line themes available at the shop. Themes are originally based on Line characters but Disney-inspired themes are now in stores as well.

Line out

If free calls is not enough to convince your pals to download Line messenger, you can still call them using Line out. You can make cheap international calls through Line out and connect to unreachable friends.
